# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Remove staff-related language from the Private Events section and add a $100 weekday discount for Monday–Thursday bookings.

**Planned changes:**
- Remove all mentions of "dedicated staff," "staff assistance," or similar language from the Private Events component; rewrite copy to reflect that guests manage their own party
- Add weekday pricing in the Private Events section showing $100 off standard prices for Monday–Thursday bookings (e.g., $550 and $700 instead of $650 and $800)
- Clearly label the weekday discount (e.g., "Weekday discount: Save $100 when you book Monday–Thursday") and keep standard pricing visible for comparison

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors viewing the Private Events section will see no mention of staff assistance and will clearly see a $100 weekday discount with both standard and discounted prices displayed.
